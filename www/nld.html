Updated OpenOffice.org packages for NLD

Where to get them

red-carpet blurb; http://go-oo.org/red-carpet/ etc. Novell-(internal) URI.

What changed

The 1.3.x versions are of the underlying build/patch infrastructure, that bears no relation to up-stream OO.o (1.1.x) release numbers. This is a simple gambit to confuse the uninitiated.
