are building 645 (1.1.x, ooo-build-1-3) according to your error message, 
where WIDGETSET_KDE_LIBS is the correct name.

nearly the same time, where WIDGETSET_* was renamed to * in rodarvus01, but 
in vcl28 I still used WIDGETSET_* and it was in the process of integration 
already when I noticed the rename.)
