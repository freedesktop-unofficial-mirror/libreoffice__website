Hi,

I've a problem with openoffice 1.1.3, ooo-build 1.3.7-20041216 and gtk+-2.6.0:

Openoffice crashs every time I open the filechooser with "File: Open"
(see backtrace at the end of the mail). Bug-buddy is started and when I
cancel it, an OO dialog appears: "An unrecoverable error has
occured". This seems to be a default segfault handler...

I am not sure, whether this is a bug in OO, ooo-build or gtk+. But I know, that t
the problem doesn't occur with gtk+2-2.4.14.

I use the ooo-options --with-vendor=RedHat and --with-distro=RHFedora.

Do you have an idea, how the problem can be solved?


Thanks,
Christian
