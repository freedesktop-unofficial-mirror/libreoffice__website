1.1.3 to circumvent the gcc 3.4 build problem. You can find ebuild and
patches in
http://darkstar.ist.utl.pt/gentoo/portage/app-office/openoffice-ximian/
or any other gentoo mirror.

With this applied, the stlport within openoffice builds fine, but
afterwards I've got caught by other problems, see my other post.
