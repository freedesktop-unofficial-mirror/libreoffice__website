	An interesting hermeneutical key I discovered in Hamburg is that an
issue being in the 'STARTED' state does not necessarily mean any work
has been started at all ;-)

	People use this to mean "I acknowledge the bug exists, confirm it & we
need to tackle it sometime" - ie. worth pinging the developer to check,
but starting work oneself on this sort of issue.
