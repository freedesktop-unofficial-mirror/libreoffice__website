Try the following sequence of operation.

a.  Run calc.
b.  Go to cell A1 and type an email-ID and press <Return>
c.  Go back to cell A1 and edit the content (in the editing panel) to
modify the email ID.  The email-ID is in blue with 'mailto' enabled. 
You would can move the cursor between the first and last character of
the email-ID and cannot modify it. (Bug 1)
d.  To modify the email-ID,  from the editing panel, cut & paste
email-ID in the same location.  You see the content doesn't turn blue in
the editing panel, but in cell A1, it becomes a superscript (Bug 2)
e.  In the Edit panel, modify the email ID and press <Return>,  the
content in A1 is cleared <Bug 3>.

Using OOo 1.1.0 ximian Edition on Xd2 box.
