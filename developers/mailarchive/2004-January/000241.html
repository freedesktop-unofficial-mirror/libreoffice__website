Yes, you can use that option.  There are a bunch more that you should 
use as well, but when you use RHFedora it will apply patches that are 
specific to what will become Red Hat's Fedora Core OOo RPM.  My job is 
to make sure that OOo works well with Fedora, and anything that I do 
that's specific to Fedora will be activated when you use 
--with-distro=RHFedora.
