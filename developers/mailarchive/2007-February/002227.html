> I'm not committing it because it's the first time something in ooo-build wants

simple solution. Patch aclocal.m4, too (better: acinclude.m4, iirc)
But why do you need PKG_CHECK_EXIST after all?

If someone wants to build with gstreamer and PKG_CHECK_MODULES fails (and
(s)he doesn't want to install gstreamer or he doesn't want to enable gstreamer
support) (s)he can use --disable-gstreamer. We should honour what people want,
otherwise they might run into the situation that they might want gstreamer,
but somehow don't have it installed and therefore don't get it.
(OK, that shopuld be seeable by the packages' contents and library deps,
but...)
