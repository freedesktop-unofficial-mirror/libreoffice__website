	I guess; still - I'd prefer to get all the symbols in there & allow
people to strip out the ones they don't want.

	It'd be pretty easy to write an 'oostrip' script that would strip
things we didn't want. A quick hack is to do:

	mkdir foo
	for a in `cat pagein-common pagein-calc`; do mv $a foo; done
	for a in *.so; do strip $a; done
	mv foo/* .

	Or something :-)
