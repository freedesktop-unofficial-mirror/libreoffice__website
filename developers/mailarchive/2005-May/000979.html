It's in CVS, 
http://cvs.gnome.org/viewcvs/ooo-build/patches/src680/fpicker-kde-resmgr.diff?rev=1.1&view=log

If you do not want to build the ooo-build HEAD from CVS, it should be enough 
to download it to your <ooo-build>/patches/src680/ subdir and add it to the 
apply file, like:

--- patches/src680/apply        26 May 2005 02:22:31 -0000      1.149
+++ patches/src680/apply        26 May 2005 10:09:59 -0000      1.151
@@ -436,6 +436,8 @@ fpicker-kde-partial-kio.diff
 # Workaround for 'Filter name (*.blah)' vs. 'Filter name' problem in
 # Insert->Picture->From File... n#66873
 fpicker-kde-filter-name.diff
+# Adapt to changes in resources handling of fpickers
+fpicker-kde-resmgr.diff

 # Let the KDE and Gtk+ fpickers build and install FIXME IZ number
 fpicker-common-scp2.diff
