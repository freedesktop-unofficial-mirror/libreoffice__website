> Q: How the **** do I make OO.o 2.0.3 (I'm downloading 2.0.4 as I write
> this) use the standard Gnome file requesters instead of its own
> proprietary, we-know-better one?

Tools->Options->OpenOffice.org->General and untick "use openoffice.org
dialogs".

AFAIK this is defaulted on for "upstream" sun-provided builds, but
defaulted off for nearly everyone else's builds.
