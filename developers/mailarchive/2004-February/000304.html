Hi,

Ok, what you'll want to do is to get the ICE.  Try this:

1) In your OOo source, add "-save-temps" to the CFLAGS line in
solver/inc/unxlngi4.mk
2) go to your OOo source dir, type "sh LinuxIntelEnv.sh" for bash, or
"source LinuxIntelEnv.set" for tcsh
3) go into i18npool, type "build.pl -P1"
4) Send mail to me with:
    a) The build log from this run including the g++ command
    b) the output of "gcc -v"
    c) Any "*.ii" and "*.s" files you might find in i18npool/
        (use 'find . -name "*.ii"' to find them, the .s shoudl be there
too)

Dan
