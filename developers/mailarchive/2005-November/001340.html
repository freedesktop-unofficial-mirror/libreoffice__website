I am not sure if I understand you correctly, but IIRC all the configure 
options that are specified to ooo-build's configure are passed to OOo's 
configure as well.

~/ooo-build$ ./configure --with-distro=BLAH --with-special-option=whatever

will result in something like

configure --option-for-distro-BLAH-1 \
          --option-for-distro-BLAH-2 ... \
          '--with-distro=BLAH' '--with-special-option=whatever'

for OOo.
