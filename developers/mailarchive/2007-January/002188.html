On Thursday 04 January 2007 02:33, Kohei Yoshida wrote:

> I was able to do a full checkout by
>
> svn co svn+ssh://(yourlogin)@svn.gnome.org/svn/ooo-build/trunk ooo-build
>
> though for some odd reason I had to type my passphrase a few times
> before the checkout actually started.

ssh-agent is your friend ;-)  Just do ssh-add, and type your passphrase, and 
you'll be always authenticated.  If ssh-agent is not running, do 'ssh-agent 
bash', and perform all the svn operations in that shell.
