On Thursday 04 November 2004 10:52, Jan Holesovsky wrote:

> I updated patches/src680 to be against src680-m60 now. The patches that
> were problematic with m60 are moved to patches/src680-m57, as well as the
> patches that are obsolete.
>
> Please note that the default tag is still src680-m57, because m60 does not
> contain 'instsetoo', just 'instsetoo_native', and I did not adapt the
> 'install' makefile rule yet. I'll announce when finished.

I made src680-m60 the default tag. You can still compile src680-m57 by 
specifying --with-tag=src680-m57 to ./configure.

'make install' does not work with m60, use the target 'rpms' instead. At the 
moment, the procedure of compiling m60 with ooo-build HEAD looks like:

./autogen.sh (add your favorite flags here)
./download
make
make rpms

Use at your own risk :-)
