Hi,


This is the first of the weekly status digest of what the Novell
OpenOffice team is up to. It will relate changes made to OpenOffice
(ooo-build or upstream) and list important work in progress.


Week Apr-30 to May-04 2007

New:
- ooo-build-2-2 branch created. ooo-build-2-2 is planned to support
oof680-m14, and the later oof680 milestones (that will lead to OOo
2.2.1).
- trunk synced to src680-m211, which has the writerfilter2 CWS  merged
in and npower6 CWS (that provide better VBA compatibility)
- fixed bug n#244914 https://bugzilla.novell.com/show_bug.cgi?id=244914
- committed the OOo smart-card patches to ooo-build from Jacob Berkman,
reported upstream: i#76870, i#76871 for integration
- Updated packages for SLED-SP1
	- http://go-oo.org/red-carpet/ooo-680/sled-10-sp-i586/
	- http://go-oo.org/red-carpet/ooo-680/sled-10-sp-x86_64/


In Progress:
- OpenXML (Excel and PowerPoint) in the xmlfilter02 CWS
- OpenXML (Word) in the writerfilter2 CWS
- More VBA support (npower7 CWS)
- Bootstrap OpenXML export. Wiki page about export:
http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Export_filter_framework
